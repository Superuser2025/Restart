## ğŸš« Part 5: Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Trading Against the Trend
**What Happens:** You see a LONG setup but Market Trend = BEARISH. You think "this time is different."

**Reality:** 90% of counter-trend trades fail. Institutions are selling, you're buying. Guess who wins?

**Fix:** ONLY trade in the direction shown on Dashboard Card 2.

---

### Mistake 2: Over-Trading
**What Happens:** You see 10 opportunities and take all of them because "more trades = more money."

**Reality:** Quality > Quantity. One perfect trade is worth 10 mediocre trades.

**Fix:** Maximum 3 trades per day. Focus on GREEN cards only.

---

### Mistake 3: Ignoring Sessions
**What Happens:** You trade EURUSD during Asian session (when Europe is sleeping).

**Reality:** Low volume = choppy movements = stop hunting = losses.

**Fix:** Only trade when Dashboard Card 3 shows London, NY, or London/NY Overlap.

---

### Mistake 4: Moving Stop Losses
**What Happens:** Trade is -30 pips, almost hitting stop. You move stop loss further away "to give it room."

**Reality:** You just increased your risk. The trade will likely hit the new stop, causing bigger losses.

**Fix:** NEVER move a stop loss to increase your risk. Set it and forget it.

---

### Mistake 5: Revenge Trading
**What Happens:** You lose 2 trades in a row. You NEED to make the money back TODAY. You take a risky trade.

**Reality:** Emotional trading = account destruction.

**Fix:** After 2 consecutive losses, STOP for the day. Come back tomorrow fresh.

---

### Mistake 6: Ignoring AI Action
**What Happens:** AI Action says "STAY OUT" but you see a GREEN card and take the trade anyway.

**Reality:** The AI is analyzing factors you can't see (exposure, market conditions, your account health). It's usually right.

**Fix:** Treat "STAY OUT" as a HARD STOP. Don't trade.

---

### Mistake 7: Trading Low-Quality Setups
**What Happens:** You see an ORANGE card (score 65) and think "this could work."

**Reality:** Your win rate on sub-70 setups will be around 40%. You'll lose money over time.

**Fix:** GREEN (85+) and BLUE (70+) only. Be patient.

---

### Mistake 8: Not Using Risk Management
**What Happens:** You risk 10% of your account on one trade because you're "really confident."

**Reality:** 5 losses in a row (which WILL happen) = -50% account. You need +100% just to break even.

**Fix:** Never risk more than 1-2% per trade. Ever. No exceptions.

## ğŸ“š Appendix: Glossary

**LPS (Last Point of Support)** - Wyckoff term for bullish reversal setup
**LPSY (Last Point of Supply)** - Wyckoff term for bearish reversal setup
**H1/H4/D1** - 1-hour, 4-hour, Daily timeframes
**Pips** - Price movement unit (0.0001 for most pairs)
**Lot** - Standard position size (1 lot = $10/pip for most pairs)
**Risk/Reward (RR)** - Ratio of potential loss to potential gain (1:2 = risk $100 to make $200)
**Stop Loss** - Pre-set price where trade automatically closes to limit loss
**Take Profit** - Pre-set price where trade automatically closes to secure profit
**ADX** - Average Directional Index (measures trend strength)
**Accumulation** - Period where institutions are BUYING (bullish)
**Distribution** - Period where institutions are SELLING (bearish)
**Confluence** - Multiple factors agreeing (e.g., trend + Wyckoff + volume)
**Ranging Market** - Price moving sideways without clear direction
**Trending Market** - Price making clear higher highs/lows (or lower highs/lows)
